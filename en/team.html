Our Leadership Committee

At OBSMUN, a panel of experienced Students take lead of the organisational and logistical aspect of the conference. In order to assure its smooth running, and as per common procedure in MUN we have a clear leadership structure which joins together to form the Leadership Committee. These Students are the key people responsible in bringing OBSMUN to life. Scroll below to discover members from each team.

Secretary General and Deputy

Our Secretary General, Pedro Pinho, and his Deputy, Matheus Drehmer, are the most important members of the Leadership Committee and are essential to the running of the conference. Both Pedro and Matheus have different leadership styles and compliment each other as the "executive duo".  Pedro is an expert in MUN Procedures and conference organisation whilst Matheus has excellent cooperation and peaceful leadership qualities.
