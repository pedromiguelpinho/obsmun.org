About Us

Attending Model United Nations Conferences is widely regarded as a highly enriching activity. Further, providing delegates with an insight into international politics and foreign diplomacy. In addition to a comprehensive understanding of the policies and efforts undertaken by international organisations such as UN subsidiaries. It does, moreover, enable the development of multiple skills ranging from debate and public speaking to negotiation and problem-solving.

Our conference is an innovative 3-day event hosting students from across the globe to partake in Committee Simulations of the United Nations. Usually occurring in the magnificent city of Oporto amidst the glamour of a historic town, the iconic OBSMUN conference aims to impregnate a rich culture of international-mindedness oriented by the philosophies of the UN in a global context, ultimately inculcating the delegates of today to become the prominent leaders of tomorrow. OBSMUN hopes to proffer a memorable and enriching experience of priceless value to all attendees.
